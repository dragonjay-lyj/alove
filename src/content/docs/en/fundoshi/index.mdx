<summary class="shadow-[0_1px_5px_rgba(0,0,0,0.03)] relative text-[1.25em] px-[1.1em] rounded-[3px] border-[none]">What you need to wear a rokushaku fundoshi</summary>

<p class="leading-[1.75] mt-0 mb-[1.6em] mx-0">Prepare a piece of cotton fabric about 30cm wide. The optimal length is 2.5m to 3.5m, so please cut it to size with scissors.</p>

<Image src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dkhp3hucr/image/upload/v1721931835/Fundoshi/0122sarashi2_lmsyg3.avif" loading="lazy" isZoomed alt="Midas" client:visible />

</details>

<p class="leading-[1.75] mt-0 mb-[1.6em] mx-0">When sporting a happi with your fundoshi, a shorter coat is considered dashing. If your happi is on the longer side,  simply fold the hem inwards for a touch of flair. Finally, secure your obi belt snugly for a polished look.</p>

<Image src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dkhp3hucr/image/upload/v1721932074/Fundoshi/shimekomi03-1_xmfqgz.avif" loading="lazy" isZoomed alt="Midas" client:visible />

<p class="center text-[xxx-large] font-[bold] text-center">How to Tie a Rokushukun<font class="text-[rgb(255,0,0)]">(Shimekomiya Style)</font></p>

<Image src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dkhp3hucr/image/upload/v1722169212/Fundoshi/simekata-01_bq0ukw.avif" loading="lazy" isZoomed alt="Midas" client:visible />

<p class="text-[26.67px]">Hold the end of the Fundoshi in your mouth (mind the length) and pass it between your legs.</p>

(Be mindful of the front and back of the fabric.)

<div class="grid gap-2 grid-cols-2">

<Image src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dkhp3hucr/image/upload/v1722169569/Fundoshi/simekata-02_klpk97.avif" loading="lazy" isZoomed alt="Midas" client:visible />

<Image src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dkhp3hucr/image/upload/v1722169569/Fundoshi/simekata-03_kgg1vv.avif" loading="lazy" isZoomed alt="Midas" client:visible />

</div>

<p class="text-[26.67px]">Twist the fabric and wrap it around your waist. (Leave it untwisted for a looser fit.)</p>

<div class="grid gap-2 grid-cols-2">

<Image src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dkhp3hucr/image/upload/v1722169928/Fundoshi/simekata-04_yvy3ns.avif" loading="lazy" isZoomed alt="Midas" client:visible />

<Image src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dkhp3hucr/image/upload/v1722169928/Fundoshi/simekata-05_ovjfgt.avif" loading="lazy" isZoomed alt="Midas" client:visible />

</div>

<p class="text-[26.67px]">Bring it to your right side, securing it with your right hand.</p>

<Image src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dkhp3hucr/image/upload/v1722170084/Fundoshi/simekata-10_jhutcx.avif" loading="lazy" isZoomed alt="Midas" client:visible />

<p class="text-[26.67px]">Release the fabric from your mouth, creating a double layer in the front. (Make sure the front side is facing out.)</p>

<div class="grid gap-2 grid-cols-2">

<Image src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dkhp3hucr/image/upload/v1722170242/Fundoshi/simekata-06_gyjccx.avif" loading="lazy" isZoomed alt="Midas" client:visible />

<Image src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dkhp3hucr/image/upload/v1722170244/Fundoshi/simekata-07_isb2w8.avif" loading="lazy" isZoomed alt="Midas" client:visible />

</div>

<p class="text-[26.67px]">Wrap the front flap (color changed for clarity) around the waist tie as you like.</p>

<div class="grid gap-2 grid-cols-2">

<Image src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dkhp3hucr/image/upload/v1722170446/Fundoshi/simekata-08_msnqhz.avif" loading="lazy" isZoomed alt="Midas" client:visible />

<Image src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dkhp3hucr/image/upload/v1722170447/Fundoshi/simekata-09_q7mwxy.avif" loading="lazy" isZoomed alt="Midas" client:visible />

</div>

<p class="text-[26.67px]">Release your right hand grip, cross the fabric, and tuck it into the waistband (with any excess length).</p>

<div class="grid gap-2 grid-cols-2">

<Image src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dkhp3hucr/image/upload/v1722170701/Fundoshi/simekata-11_k6gzty.avif" loading="lazy" isZoomed alt="Midas" client:visible />

</div>

 <p class="text-[26.67px]">Adjust the front flap and you're done! </p>
